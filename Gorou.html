Gorou

General of Watsumi

Brief information

Gorou is a playable Geo character in Genshin Impact.
He is the courageous and reliable general of the Watatsumi Army, a leader whose men can always place their trust in.





Gorou can deploy only 1 General's War Banner on the field at any one time.

After using Juuga: Forward Unto Victory, all nearby party members' DEF is increased by 25% for 12s.

Increases the Level of Juuga: Forward Unto Victory by 3. This effect cannot stack and will take reference from the last instance of the effect that is triggered.
